## Molecular Force Search Optimizer
This is the source code of Molecular Force Search Optimizer written in MATLAB.
A new meta-heuristic optimization algorithm, the Molecular Force Search Optimizer (MFSO), 
is proposed based on the law of molecular interactions. MFSO simulates the interaction laws 
between molecules, divides the search space into the equilibrium stage (exploitation), the 
attractive stage (exploration), and the repulsive stage (exploitation), and designs a balance 
circle and a distance ratio factor to dynamically transition the exploration and utilization stages in the search space.
